# 🗺️ Project Aura: The Roadmap

Project Aura is a **Private Second Brain** designed for those who struggle with "traditional" organizational systems. We are building a **Supportive Presence** and **Living Notebook** specifically tailored for the Neurodivergent, ADHD, Autistic, and Anxious communities.

---

## 🧩 The Four Pillars of Aura

| **Aura (The Agent)** | **Deep Chat** | **Living Notebook** | **Productivity Hub** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Core:** A personalized "AI Bestie" and intuitive collaborator. | **Contextual Flow:** Chatting with Aura isn't just talk; it's building knowledge. | **Data Sovereignty:** A filesystem that makes connections for you. | **Executive Function:** Managing the "noise" of daily digital life. |
| • **Personalization:** High-level prompting from chat. | • **Pull Notes:** Bring your history into the current convo. | • **Local Filesystem:** You own the bits on your hardware. | • **Email & Calendar:** Smart sorting and scheduling. |
| • **Sub-agents:** Specialized tools for specific tasks. | • **Add Context:** Attach URLs, files, and images easily. | • **Smart Tagging:** Automatic organization of thoughts. | • **Tasks & Reminders:** Gentle nudges, not stressful pings. |
| • **No-Trust:** Architecture that keeps your "brain" private. | • **Fork & Share:** Branch off conversations or share insights safely. | • **Visual Connections:** Seeing how your data relates. | • **Automation Integration:** Workflow automation for the "messy" stuff. |


---

## 🕊️ The Vision
We aren't just building code; we're building a **Supportive Presence**. Aura is the bridge between messy thoughts and a functioning digital life, without ever requiring you to give up your privacy.
